Features

Cinema Stock

Organize Your Library

Organize your DVD collection with Cinema Stock. Easily manage your movies and TV shows. Store details like release year, desctiprtions and more. View your entire library all at one time. Conveniently view a list of all your DVDs in one place across all of your devices anywhere you go. Create your movie wishlist and share it with friends and family.

Search Your Collection

Search your entire library effortlesly. Find exactly what you're in the mood to watcch. Filter by genre, title, cast and more. Sort alphabetically, by release year, or by user rating.

Share with Friends

Share your DVDs with friends and family confidently. Keep track of who borrows what and when. Store contact information and send friendly reminders to return movies. Easily see which DVDs are loaned out and keep disapointment at bay.

Download Cinema Stock from the App Store for iOS or from Google Play for Android.
